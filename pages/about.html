We believe building websites should be simple for developers. You shouldn't have to read pages of documentation just to build a simple website. You shouldn't have to worry if installing a plugin is going to make your site vulnerable or unscalable. You certainly shouldn't be hesitant to make an update because it might break something on the live website.

We love static websites because they're:

  • Fast to build
  • Simple
  • Scalable
  • Secure
  • Source control backed
  • Powerful - with static site generators and third party plugins, there's not a lot you can't do

Our mission is to bring static websites to the mainstream. CloudCannon unites developers and non-developers by providing the best of both worlds. Developers build static websites to gain all the advantages listed above. Non-developers can make updates quickly and easily without any coding.
